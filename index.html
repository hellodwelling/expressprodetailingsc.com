About Us

Welcome to Express Pro Detailing! We are a family-owned and operated detailing company servicing areas such as Kiawah Island, John's Island, Edisto Island, Isle of Palms and all surrounding beaches and Islands of Charleston, South Carolina.

We offer professional detailing services for a wide range of transportation vehicles such as Automobiles (of all types), Marine ( Of All Types), Rv's, Buses, Atv's, Motorcycles (All Types), & All small privately owned Aviation Aircrafts.

We Specialize In Marine Gel Coat Restoration & Cleaning Of All Marine Vessels, Automotive Paint Correction & Restoration, Rv And Bus Paint Correction & Restoration & Aviation Detailing and Ceramic Coating. We pride ourselves on delivering the best service for your needs. Please give us a call at (864) 518-3459 for a free estimate today!

Our Services

What we are best at - expert base-detail

Paint Correction

Swirls, scratches, oxidation, water spots, and chemical etching can cause your paint to look dull and faded. We'll help you correct all these blemishes and get your vehicle looking new again.

Interior Detailing

We can help you clean your interiors to help remove stains, dirt, and other debris from daily use. Our interior cleaning includes carpet cleaning, steam cleaning, and vacuuming of floor mats, dashboards, consoles, vents, steering wheels, and much more.

Exterior Detailing

Keep your vehicle looking brand new with our exterior cleaning services. For our exterior cleaning, we include a foam cannon wash, bug and tar removal, wax (or sealant), wheels and tire cleaning, window and glass cleaning, and more.

Maintenance Wash

Keep your vehicle looking brand new with our maintenance wash. Our experienced staff will arrive quickly and get the job done right, every time.

Ceramic Coating

Innovative technology allows us to apply a 9H ceramic coating to help protect your vehicle from bird etchings, water spotting, and harsh environmental elements leaving a high gloss finish with unmatched water beading technology. Protect your investment and keep your vehicle looking better than when you bought it.

Full Detail Service

For cars, trucks and other vehicles we offer full service detailing to make your vehicle look new again! Interior and exterior detailing available.
